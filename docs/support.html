Common questions

How do I change which categories I play?

Open the Profile tab and tap Change under your account. You can turn categories on or off at any time — your choices update right away.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email. We'll send you a link to set a new one. If you signed up with Google, use Google's account recovery instead — GenTogether doesn't store a separate password for Google accounts.

I'm playing as a guest. Will I lose my progress?

Guest progress is tied to your device. To keep your progress if you switch devices or reinstall the app, create a permanent account from the Profile tab before that happens.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at liying.chen@student.uts.edu.au from the address associated with your account and we'll delete your account and data.
