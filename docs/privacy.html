GenTogether ("the app," "we," "us") is a mobile app that helps people practice spotting AI-generated images. This page explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and who it's shared with.

Information we collect

When you use GenTogether, we collect:

  • Account information. If you continue as a guest, we create an anonymous identifier for your device with no personal details attached. If you choose to create a permanent account, we collect the email address (or Google account information) you use to sign up.
  • Gameplay data. The challenge categories you choose to play, and your progress and scores as you complete challenges.

We don't collect your real name, location, contacts, or payment information.

How we use this information

  • To save your progress and preferences between sessions
  • To show you challenges from the categories you've chosen to play
  • To let you sign in to the same account across devices, if you've created a permanent account

Services we use

GenTogether relies on the following third-party services to operate. Each handles data under its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google). Handles sign-in and stores your account and gameplay data.
  • Supabase. Hosts the images and videos used in the app's challenges. This is app content, not personal data.

Your choices

You can change your challenge category preferences at any time from the Profile tab. If you'd like your account and associated data deleted, contact us using the details below and we'll remove it.

Children's privacy

GenTogether is not directed at children, and we don't knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we'll delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
